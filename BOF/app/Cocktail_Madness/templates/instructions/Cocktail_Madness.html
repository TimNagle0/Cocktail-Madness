This is a game where you have to serve customers the cocktails that they order. You do this by adding ingredients into the cocktail shaker, shaking it and then serving it to the right customer. You can add ingredients to the shaker by pressing the right hotkey (q,w,e,r,a,s,d), shake it by mashing spacebar as quickly as possible, and serve it to the right customer using 1,2,3 & 4


If you want to clear the shaker at any point, you can do so by pressing "T".
